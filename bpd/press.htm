Here at Broadview we pride ourselves on our hard work and creative thinking. This makes it so that we receive recognition from other organizations and groups. In this section we would like to outline various awards and recognitions.

   
WMSBF Recognizes Broadview!
   

West Michigan Sustainable Business Forum recognizes Broadview Product Development for its' contribution as part of a 4 member team effort to support their goal in promoting Environmental, Economic and Social responsibility. Using LED lighting technology, green materials, ease of assembly and disassembly design methods the team's efforts support efficiencies in energy, manufacturing, packaging and shipping.
